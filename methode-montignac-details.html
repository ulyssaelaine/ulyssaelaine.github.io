Méthode Montignac pour maigrir

Project information

  • Category: iOS Mobile App
  • Company: Anxa Limited
  • Project date: Mar 2017 - Present
  • Project URL: https://apps.apple.com/fr/app/m%C3%A9thode-montignac-pour-maigrir/id370539329

• Developed a diet coaching mobile app based on Montignac diet, a high-protein low-carbohydrate fad diet that was popular in the 1990s, mainly in Europe. Through the app, the users can interact with the coach and send a personal message. The app also features daily personalized meal plans and a hundred of healthy recipes.
• The user can also sync his steps (inluding duration and kilometer(s)) taken through the Apple HealthKit or Google Fit. He can also input his daily weight and daily meals using the app.
• France App Store Rating: 4.5/5.0

• Technology Used: Swift (iOS), Apple HealthKit framework, Push Notification, In-App Purchase
