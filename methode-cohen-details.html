• Developed a diet coaching mobile app under Dr Jean Michel Cohen, a French nutritionist. Through the app, the users can interact with the coach and send a personal message. The app also features health-related video tips, daily personalized meal plans, a hundred of slimming recipes and also easy exercises to do at home.
• The user can also sync his steps (inluding duration and kilometer(s)) taken through the Apple HealthKit or Google Fit. He can also input his daily weight and daily meals using the app.
• France App Store Rating: 4.5/5.0
• France Google Playstore Rating: 4.1/5.0

• Technology Used: React Native (iOS and Android), Apple HealthKit framework, Push Notification, In-App Purchase
